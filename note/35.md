# 35、访问列表元素（2018-04-02，0.5h）



###课程目标：

* 按顺序访问列表元素（序数 and 基数）



###课程内容：

```
animals = ['bear', 'python', 'peacock', 'kangaroo', 'whale', 'platypus']

print " The animal at 0 is the 1st animal and is a  %r " % animals[0]
print " The animal at 1 is the 2sed animal and is a %r " % animals[1] 
print " The animal at 2 is the 3rd animal and is a %r " % animals[2]
print " The animal at 3 is the 4th animal and is a %r " % animals[3]
print " The animal at 4 is the 5th animal and is a %r " % animals[4]
print " The animal at 5 is the 6th animal and is a %r " % animals[5]
```

返回结果：

```
 The animal at 0 is the 1st animal and is a  'bear' 
 The animal at 1 is the 2sed animal and is a 'python' 
 The animal at 2 is the 3rd animal and is a 'peacock' 
 The animal at 3 is the 4th animal and is a 'kangaroo' 
 The animal at 4 is the 5th animal and is a 'whale' 
 The animal at 5 is the 6th animal and is a 'platypus' 
```
